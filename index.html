I'm Pratik Javiya — a social media and marketing manager specialising in UAE-based retail, home, and lifestyle brands. My work sits at the intersection of cultural storytelling, data-led strategy, and bold creative direction.

I've worked across some of the Gulf region's most recognised retail Instagram communities — from Home Centre's 2M-strong audience to Nice Stores' 1M followers and 9,853-post archive, Ajmal Perfumes' 321K fragrance-lovers, and Homebox AE's growing 241K community.

From Ramadan activations that drove record engagement to luxury fragrance launches built on sensory storytelling, every campaign I shape is designed with one purpose: to connect, convert, and endure.
